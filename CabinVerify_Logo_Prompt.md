# CabinVerify Logo Generation Prompts
**Use in: Grok Imagine, Gemini Image Gen, Midjourney, Adobe Firefly, or similar**

---

## Prompt 1 — Ridge Check (Recommended Concept)

> Minimal professional logo for a company called CabinVerify. The icon is a clean, geometric mountain ridgeline silhouette with two or three peaks rendered in simple flat vector style. The rightmost peak subtly incorporates a checkmark shape — the peak itself forms the upward stroke of a check, making the ridge and the verification mark the same element. Below the icon, the wordmark reads "CabinVerify" in a bold, modern sans-serif font. Primary icon color is deep forest green hex #1C3829. Wordmark color is near-black hex #1F2937. Optional small tagline below in antique amber hex #9C7A3C reading "Local eyes. Neutral proof." White background. Flat vector style, no gradients, no shadows, no textures. Clean, scalable, corporate-level simplicity. The overall feel is calm neutral authority — not outdoorsy adventure brand, not home repair company. Think BBB or CARFAX visual weight but with a Smoky Mountain identity. No people, no tools, no buildings, no magnifying glass.

## Prompt 2 — Shield Cabin (Alternative Concept)

> Minimal professional logo for a company called CabinVerify. The icon is a clean geometric shield outline in flat vector style. Inside the shield, centered, is a very minimal cabin silhouette — just the essential A-frame or gabled roofline with a single vertical chimney, reduced to its simplest geometric form. No doors, windows, or details. The shield communicates neutral verification authority. The cabin communicates the short-term rental context. Below the icon, the wordmark reads "CabinVerify" in bold Montserrat-style sans-serif. Primary colors: deep forest green #1C3829 for the shield and icon elements, near-black #1F2937 for the wordmark text. White or transparent background. Flat vector, no gradients, no 3D effects, no drop shadows. Corporate-quality simplicity — calm, neutral, trustworthy. Not rustic, not folksy, not adventurous. Similar visual weight to a professional certification or accreditation logo. No people, no tools, no repair imagery.

## Prompt 3 — Icon Only (Favicon / App Icon Version)

> Minimal flat vector app icon for a company called CabinVerify. Square icon with slightly rounded corners. Background is deep forest green #1C3829. Centered in the icon is a white geometric mountain ridgeline with two clean peaks. The right peak doubles as a checkmark — its descending right stroke curves like the bottom of a check mark. Pure white icon on deep green background. No text, no gradients, no textures, no shadows. Clean enough to read at 32x32 pixels. Think Apple App Store icon quality. Professional, minimal, modern.

## Tips for Getting the Best Result

- **Run Prompt 1 and Prompt 2** and compare — one will feel more "you" when you see them side by side
- **Reject any output with**: hammers, clipboards, magnifying glasses, hard hats, orange colors, clip-art style illustrations, or heavily textured/rustic effects
- **Keep any output that**: reads as calm authority, looks like it could be a BBB-adjacent trust mark, and still hints at mountains/cabin without screaming "hiking brand"
- **Iterate by adding**: "make the peaks more angular and minimal" or "reduce the shield to a simpler outline" or "make the wordmark heavier/lighter"
- **For Midjourney**: add `--style raw --ar 3:1` for horizontal logo layout, or `--ar 1:1` for icon-only square
- **For Adobe Firefly**: select "Graphic Design → Logo" as the content type before entering the prompt

## Color Reference for Logo Review

When reviewing generated options, verify against these:

| Element | Correct Color | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Primary icon / dark fill | Ridge Night | `#1C3829` |
| Wordmark text | Deep Hollow | `#1F2937` |
| Tagline / accent | Appalachian Amber | `#9C7A3C` |
| Background | Summit White | `#FFFFFF` |
| Icon on dark version | Summit White | `#FFFFFF` |

If the generator outputs a different green, note the hex and compare visually — slight variation is okay as long as it reads as deep forest green (dark, muted, professional). Reject anything that looks lime green, sage green, or olive.
